What Does a VSO Do?
Build Your Claim Help you gather the right medical evidence, nexus letters, buddy statements, and service records before you file.
File on Your Behalf Submit claims, supplemental evidence, and appeals directly with the VA as your accredited representative.
Fight Denials Guide you through Supplemental Claims, Higher-Level Reviews, and Board of Veterans Appeals.
Always Free Federal law prohibits VSOs from charging for claims help. If someone asks for money, walk away.
